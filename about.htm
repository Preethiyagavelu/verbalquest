Our Story

We started this project with a passion for helping students and professionals enhance their verbal reasoning skills. Seeing the demand for logical reasoning tests in competitive exams and job interviews, we decided to create a free, engaging, and interactive platform for learning. This altruistic project is our way of contributing to the education community.
